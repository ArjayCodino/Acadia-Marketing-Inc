  • Age- Even the most well-maintained roofs will eventually wear out over time. Most roofs have a lifespan of between 15 and 25 years, depending on the materials used. As a roof ages, it becomes more vulnerable to damage from the elements.
  • Weather- North Hollywood is known for its dry weather, but that doesn't mean the weather can't cause damage to your roof. Strong winds, hail, and heavy rainstorms can all cause damage to shingles, tiles, and other roofing materials.
  • Trees and branches- Trees can provide much-needed shade in North Hollywood, but they can also cause damage to roofs. Overhanging branches can scrape against the roof and damage shingles, while falling limbs can cause significant damage to the roof structure.
